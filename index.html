Select 9 letters by clicking the buttons, no more than 6 consonants and no more than 5 vowels. Then find the longest word you can spell with the available letters. Countdown begins as soon as you select the 9th letter.

Select 6 numbers and a target number will be randomly generated. Using the numbers you chose (each number only once) and simple artithmetic (addition, multiplication, subtraction, and division), construct an equation that equals the target number or as close to it as possible. Countdown begins as soon as the 6th number is selected. If you are unsure what to pick, 2 big numbers and 4 small numbers are recommended.
